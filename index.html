The Artist
The story behind the lens and the sound.
Self Portrait — 2025

Between shutter
and silence

Photographer · Musician · Visual Storyteller

I've always been drawn to the in-between — the moment after the crowd disperses, the light just before it fades, the note that hangs in the air a beat too long.

Photography and music aren't separate pursuits for me; they're two halves of the same impulse — an attempt to hold onto something that's already slipping away.
